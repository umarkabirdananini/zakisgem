Who are we?

An NGO that promotes the fundamental human rights and general welfare of the youth by encouraging the acceptance and practice of child related laws in collaboration with community stakeholders through advocacy, social mobilization and capacity building.
the foundation was created on 18th October, 2011 and registered with CAC on 05th March, 2020.

Core Values

the core values of ZGSF include Accountability, Integrity, Transparency, Justice, Peace and Sustainability with strategic focus on Results Oriented Advocacy, Capacity Development, Service Delivery, and Outreaches.

Scope

We work in various thematic areas which includes Building Social Capital, Gender Based Violence, Livelihood, Health and Education.
